Trustworthy Advice, Exceptional Service.

Independent advisors, Jerod Wurm, CFP® and Casey Frye, CFP® are passionately committed to helping individuals, families and foundations plan for the future through a culture of trustworthy advice and exceptional service. Accordingly, your needs, and those of your family, are met by working directly with Jerod and Casey, the Principals of our team. As fiduciaries, we act solely for your benefit as a guardian and steward of your wealth. We are here to serve you with regularly scheduled calls, news, emails and in-person meetings.

We are:

  • An independent, client-centric wealth management firm conveniently located in Roseville.
  • Focused on family investors who demand excellence in their investment activities.
  • Experienced advisors who specialize in helping to grow, protect and distribute investment assets prudently.
  • Dedicated to our approach of helping clients define their long-term investment objectives and build personalized investment portfolios designed to achieve them.
  • Fiercely proud of our role as independent advisors and look forward to introducing you to the benefits of strategic investment planning and comprehensive wealth management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide strategic investment planning and diversified portfolio management and provide trustworthy advice to families and foundations with our commitment to exceptional service and guidance through meaningful and timely communication.
